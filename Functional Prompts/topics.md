### **Task-Oriented/Functional Prompts**:

1. **Text Summarization**:
   - **Label**: **Summarization Prompt**
   - **Description**: Prompts designed to condense a longer piece of text into a shorter summary while retaining the key points and essential information. These prompts guide the AI to extract the most important content and present it concisely.
   - **Example**: "Summarize the following article in three sentences."

2. **Inference**:
   - **Label**: **Inference Prompt**
   - **Description**: Prompts that ask the AI to draw conclusions or make predictions based on given information. These are used to perform logical reasoning, deducing implicit information from explicit data.
   - **Example**: "Given the following evidence, what can you infer about the situation?"

3. **Transforming**:
   - **Label**: **Transformation Prompt**
   - **Description**: Prompts that involve changing the format, structure, or style of the input text. This could include converting text from one format to another, translating languages, or rephrasing text while maintaining the original meaning.
   - **Example**: "Rewrite the following sentence in passive voice."

### **Other Related Labels**:

4. **Classification**:
   - **Label**: **Classification Prompt**
   - **Description**: Prompts that ask the AI to categorize or label input data into predefined classes or categories.
   - **Example**: "Classify the following text as positive, negative, or neutral."

5. **Question Answering**:
   - **Label**: **Question-Answering Prompt**
   - **Description**: Prompts designed to elicit specific answers to questions posed within the prompt, often based on provided context or a dataset.
   - **Example**: "What is the capital of France?"

6. **Generation**:
   - **Label**: **Generation Prompt**
   - **Description**: Prompts that instruct the AI to generate new content based on given input. This could include creating stories, generating code, or writing essays.
   - **Example**: "Write a short story about a journey to Mars."

7. **Translation**:
   - **Label**: **Translation Prompt**
   - **Description**: Prompts that instruct the AI to convert text from one language to another, maintaining the original meaning and context.
   - **Example**: "Translate the following sentence from English to Spanish."

8. **Summarization**:
   - **Label**: **Summarization Prompt**
   - **Description**: Prompts that guide the AI to condense information into a shorter form while retaining the key messages.
   - **Example**: "Summarize the main points of this meeting transcript."

9. **Comparison**:
   - **Label**: **Comparison Prompt**
   - **Description**: Prompts that require the AI to compare two or more pieces of data or text and highlight similarities or differences.
   - **Example**: "Compare the economic policies of the two countries mentioned."

10. **Conversion**:
    - **Label**: **Conversion Prompt**
    - **Description**: Prompts that involve converting one type of data or text into another format, such as transforming a list into a paragraph.
    - **Example**: "Convert the following bullet points into a coherent paragraph."
