# Learning OUTCOMES

1. **Understand Prompt Engineering**: Learners will be able to explain the concept of prompt engineering, including its purpose, key components, and how it influences the behavior and outcomes of AI models.
2. **Design Effective Prompts**: Learners will be able to design effective prompts that leverage context, structure, and specific instructions to consistently achieve the desired results from AI models.
3. **Utilize Prompt Patterns**: Learners will be able to utilize different prompt patterns, including understanding their purpose, structure, and when to use them to achieve specific AI outputs.
4. **Optimize Prompts**: Learners will be able to optimize prompts to achieve specific outcomes.
5. **Evaluate and Refine Prompts**: Learners will be able to evaluate and refine their prompts based on AI responses.
6. **Understand AI Interpretation**: Learners will understand the underlying principles of how AI interprets prompts.
